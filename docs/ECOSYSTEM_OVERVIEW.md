# Healthcare AI Ecosystem Overview

## Vision
Every person has instant access to quality healthcare. Every outbreak is detected before becoming an epidemic.

## Two Integrated Solutions
1. **Healthzy (SAAKSHYA)**: An AI Personal Health Assistant for individuals.
2. **SYMPTOMAP**: A Real-Time Disease Surveillance platform for healthcare professionals and systems.

## Bidirectional Integration
- **Individual to Population**: Data flows from individual symptom reports (SAAKSHYA) to inform population surveillance trends.
- **Population to Individual**: Real-time warnings flow from the surveillance system (SYMPTOMAP) back to individuals in affected areas, enabling early prevention.

## Social Impact Goal
Achieve a 30% reduction in preventable deaths by 2030 through early detection, rapid outbreak response, and proactive intervention, especially in low-resource settings.
